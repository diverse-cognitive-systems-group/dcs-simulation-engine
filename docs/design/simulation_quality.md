Ensuring that DCS-SE produces consistently high-quality role-playing behavior constitutes a central design principle. 

Accordingly, simulation quality is serves as a foundational evaluation and is systematically re-conducted following modifications to characters or underlying models. 

This study is designed to assess the representational fidelity of core DCS-SE characters, identify potential failure modes, and evaluate the reliability of internal quality assessment mechanisms.

The results for the current engine version are available here: [DCS-SE Simulation Quality Report](../reports/simulation_quality_report.html)
